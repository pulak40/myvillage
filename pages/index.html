Team HABLU

Who we are

We are team HABLU. Looking around the world, and its current COVID19 situation, we are inspired to do something for our village. Being village growing kid, we are always looking for doing something good for our area. But with limited opportunity and resource our idea never flourish. But due to COVID19, we got an unique opportunity to come together and do something good for our community.

Our Mission

We are living at unique time of our era. We never thought that we will be in locked down behind the walls for these many days. Being in this state, it helped us redifne our way of think and acting in the world. Being in IT, we came across an unique idea to do something for the greater good for the community. We are looking forward to help people with multitude of information and make there day to day life with ease of access to anything possible.

Our Vission

Looking at how the technological era changing our day to day world, we are very optimistic. Technological advancement is the key aspect for multidimensional progress in human race, whether it is agriculture or automobiles, spacecraft etc. almost in every direction.We are aiming towards MARS, that itself sound like dreaming for us just a decade ago.

With technical advancement we have reached in a new high. But like every high there are some low also, we have to look forward to solve our social aspect also. With technological advancement, we should also look for better human being socially and culturally evolved being.
