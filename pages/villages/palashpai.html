Palashpai is a village and a gram panchayat in Daspur II Block in Ghatal subdivision of Paschim Medinipur district in the state of West Bengal, India.

Geography

There are six villages under Palashpai gram panchayat: Ajurya, Bhagaban Chak, Jayram Chak, Jayrampur, Jot Keshab and Palashpai.

Demographics

As per 2011 Census of India Palashpai had a total population of 4,800 of which 2,459 were males and 2,341 were females.
